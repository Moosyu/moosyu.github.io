10(+) mods Moosyu can't live without:

Skytils

1.8.9 Best Skyblock mod at the moment, clean GUI, lots of good features and my favourite solvers, easy to use waypoint system for mining.

Patcher

1.8.9 & 1.12.2 Fixes some issues with farming (something to do with wheat height I dunno) in 1.8.9 and (allegedly) increases performance according to some guy who told me like three years ago. Really no reason not to have.

Sidebar Mod

1.8.9 Quite unsure as to what this mod actually does. I picked it up in some BEST SKYBLOCK MODS video a few years ago and I just keep it on in case its secretly keeping everything together somehow.

Scrollable Tooltips

1.8.9 - 1.12.2 Very simple but helpful mod, just lets you scroll tooltips to be able to read long ass item names.

Autotip

1.8.9 - 1.19.1 Basically just gives you free Hypixel exp. I've left this mod on for a few years and I'm level 100 and something without actually grinding for anything or using boosters.

Danker's Skyblock Mod

1.8.9 Another great multipurpose Skyblock mod like Skytils. The UI is awful, I don't know why he had to change it from the nice one DSM had a year or two back but apart from that it's got lots of cool features though the only ones I actually use are the trackers, drop alerts, different enchant colours and some of the features taken from SBA.

Dungeon Rooms Mod

1.8.9 Nice to have when you're trying to memorise secret locations or even if you have memorised them and just have a mind blank and forget.

Not Enough Updates

1.8.9 Up there with Skytils in just quantity of stuff. From slot locking to /pv to fairysoul waypoints to treecap cooldowns to a way better storage gui overhaul, NEU is pretty essential for me.

SkyHanni

1.8.9 New(ish) addition to my mod loadout. I mainly use SkyHanni for farming but I keep finding awesome features like that Diana spot finder, actual game changer.

Optifine

1.7.2 - 1.21.4 The OG performance mod. I luckily don't have a PC so shit I actually need it but zooming is nice and I bought a cape for some reason so I need to get my moneys worth. I hear later versions of the mod aren't so great but for 1.8.9 it's just fine.

Recipe Browser

1.4.4.9 Super helpful when you don't want to open the wiki to find out what mod something is from or how to get it.

Boss Checklist

1.4.4.9 Feels like it should just be a feature in the base game? Super helpful for figuring out which boss is next, what you can expect from it and how to craft it again without needing to minimise the game and go to the wiki.

Boss Cursor

1.4.4.9 Pretty simple UI mod that points to where the boss you're fighting is so it's less easy to get jumped out of nowhere if you don't want to be scanning your map.

Fargo's Mutant Mod

1.4.4.9 Takes a lot of the tedium out of repeat playthroughs with a few extra NPC's who sell spawn items to rare enemies, boss summons and items to instantly craft structures like hellbridges and NPC homes.

Vein Miner

1.4.4.9 Speaks for itself, it lets you mine ores faster. You can actually set it to mine anything which is a bit cheaty but the tradeoff is that when you mine 10000 sand at once your pc blows up.

Magic Storage

1.4.4.9 Takes out the tedium of hunting through dozens of chests with a simple chest system along with a way easier way to craft with materials you have.

Alchemist NPC Lite

1.4.4.9 NPC that sells potions. Saves you from the most pointless tedious part of the game.

Shorter Respawn Time

1.4.4.9 A big cheaty in multiplayer but in singleplayer it just feels right.

No More Tombs

1.4.4.9 No more grave clean up for me!!

Begone, Evil!

1.4.4.9 For some reason the evil biomes spreading is really stressful for me and since I don't like being wracked with stress while playing video games this one is pretty necessary.

Wing Slot Extra

1.4.4.9 Probably the most cheaty mod on this list because it's in effect just giving you an extra accessory slot but it feels so right.

Fargo's Souls Mod

1.4.4.9 Unique and fun content mod, not the most over the top bosses or anything (except for a few pretty standout exceptions) but certainly over the top in the insane ass accessories that set my PC alight. There's also a DLC for this that adds Calamity support which is awesome.

Calamity

1.4.4.9 Most replayable content mod I've ever used. So much extra content that I find myself coming back over and over despite having so many content mods I've never played.
