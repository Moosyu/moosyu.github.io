    liveserver (the vscode) extension doesnt work with imgur for some reason idk?

  • first host your site

  • then on vscode read whatever port it tells you that your server is hosting on (it shows that in the same spot it previously said go live before clicking it)

  • go into your browser and enter localhost:5501 and replace the 5501 with whatever your port says

    • problem solved!
