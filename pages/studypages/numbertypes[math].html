number types!!

prime numbers: numbers whose only factors are 1 and itself

2,3,5,7,11,13,17,19,23

composite numbers: numbers with more than two factors

4,6,8,9,10,12,14,15,16

square numbers: numbers that are the result of squaring a number

eg: 1 is sqrt of 1, 4 is sqrt of 2, 9 is sqrt of 3

cube numbers: same as square numbers but cubed

1, 8, 27, 64, 125
